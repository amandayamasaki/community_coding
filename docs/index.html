North Lawndale, the opportunity of life and freedom.

Our goal is to use technology to change how the rest of Chicago views the West Side.

We wanted to create a website for the community where people can have access to events, resources, and information about what goes on in North Lawndale. We hope to give the community a voice to show that there are many positive things and wonderful opportunities not just in North Lawndale, but the whole West Side.
