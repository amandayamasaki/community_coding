T.R.A.C.E ( Teens Reimagining Art, Community, and Environment )

Instagram: @teensreimaginin

T.R.A.C.E is a program run by the Chicago Park District. They meet in Hamilton Park on the South Side of Chicago in Englewood.

This organization brings South Side teens together as creative artists and educators. These “TRACErs” collaborate with the community to design and compose art. To celebrate their 10th anniversary, they have designed a mural on 58th and Halsted. This is to celebrate the work they’ve accomplished over the past decade, and also recognize their supporters and celebrate all past, present, and future Tracers. Lawndale Coding collaborated with them by making a QR code to send the viewers to information about the figures in their mural.
